Book review : " In Reckless Hands " looks at Oklahoma 's eugenics history The state of Oklahoma shares a dark past with Adolf Hitler and Third Reich . Eugenics , the science hailed by academics worldwide as a means to improve a race by sterilizing those who had heredity defects , was also embraced by Governor Alfalfa Bill Murray. " In Reckless Hands : Skinner v. Oklahoma and the Near-Triumph of American Eugenics , " by Victoria F. Nourse , probes how a scientific theory transformed into the basis of social policy ordained by the power of the state of Oklahoma. The nazi programs were eventually forcefully stopped , but in U.S. over 100,000 people had been sterilized under federal programs - even as late as the 1960s . ( newsok.com )
