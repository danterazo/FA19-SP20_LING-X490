The Bruders Schweigen , the Silent Brotherhood , or the Order however you 'd like to refer to it , is a story unto itself. Since though , it played such an instrumental role in David 's life , I will mention it as best I can here. On September 22 , 1983 Robert Mathews invited nine men to join him for a meeting in a building on his property near Metaline Falls , Washington. Ten chairs stood in a circle , the men filling 9 of them and a picture of the Fuhrer filling the tenth , soon after Mathews brought a white baby to the center of the circle and the men took an oath , swearing to secure the future of that white child .
