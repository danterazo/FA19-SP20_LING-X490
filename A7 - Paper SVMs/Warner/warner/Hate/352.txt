Why do Asians miscegenate in such large numbers ? I want to illuminate one reason ( among many ) : we are the most naive and sheeplike of all the races. We accept and practice the ideologies that are presented to us in the media and in the educational system , without understanding that these are radical ideas practiced only by a tiny minority of people , and that the vast majority of regular white people do not adhere to them , even if they accept them superficially. For example , we frequently hear that whites encourage their children to date and marry anyone , no matter their color , or creed , or whatnot , whereas Asian families " refuse to accept " interracial dating or marriage. This belief in the holiness of race-mixing is pounded into our skulls every day , especially on TV and in film , and in the print media. And yet how many whites actually engage in this behavior ? Census Bureau data indicate that , in a country that 's 35 % non-white , roughly 97 % of all married whites are married to other whites . The truth is that there is still resistance to race-mixing within the white community , even in this age of multicultural enlightenment. The other day , a ( white ) friend of mine explained to me that his father had told his sister that she ever even dated a black male , he 'd kill him on the spot. When 's the last time you heard about an Asian father saying analogously to his daughter ?
