Approximately three years after Nathan and Alyza Lewin led the Bryan v . Moore amicus curiae brief another notable event took place. A kosher dinner was held to honor the establishment of the National Institute for Judaic Law ( NIJL ) . The dinner was attended by 200 people , including Supreme Court Justices Ruth Bader Ginsberg , Stephen Breyer , Antonin Scalia. Nathan and Alyza Lewin also attended .
