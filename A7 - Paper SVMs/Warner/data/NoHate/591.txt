Eventually it became clear that " integration " and " assimilation " would not , could not , occur. By the time this was obvious to all , the alien presence was ( at least politically ) irreversible. There was therefore a shift in political policy. The word " diversity " was now used in a pitiful exercise of political face-saving. The future of Europe was now projected by political spin to become one of " diversity " . This word , " diversity " , became the banner of a new political crusade .
